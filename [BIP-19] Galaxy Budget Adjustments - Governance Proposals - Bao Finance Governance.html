[BIP-19] Galaxy Budget Adjustments

Governance Proposals
Jester September 9, 2022, 8:13pm 1

We started with small galaxy operating funds when we defined our process to standardize and streamline contributions. However, we found out that the initial monthly budget could not cover the cost of 1 or 2 full-time contributors and that if someone contributed to that extent, it would have to be paid by the treasury itself and not by the galaxy that person is contributing to.

If the situation arises, this proposal seeks to give more space to reward one or two full-time contributors.

The initial proposal gives a monthly budget to the following Galaxies:

  • Maintainer Galaxy (5k)
  • Community Galaxy (2.5k)
  • Front-End Galaxy (2.5k)
  • Smart Contract Galaxy (5k)
  • Quality Assurance Galaxy (1k)
  • Art Galaxy (1k)

The proposal seeks to set the monthly budget to the following galaxies:

  • Maintainer Galaxy (5k)
  • Community Galaxy (5k)
  • Front-End Galaxy (10k)
  • Smart Contract Galaxy (15k)
  • Quality Assurance Galaxy (2k)
  • Art Galaxy (2k)

The same rules on restriction over the Galaxy budget remain. Operating budgets are additive, meaning that if the monthly allocation is not spent, the remaining balance will roll over to the new allocation. However, the operating budget accumulated shall not be allowed to exceed three times the monthly budget. New funds will halt until the budget is being spent again.
