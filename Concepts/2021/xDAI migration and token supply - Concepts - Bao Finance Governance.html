suffex57 February 18, 2021, 3:07pm 4

No problem! When tokens from ethereum is transferred to xDai and vice versa, they are not duplicated. I don‚Äôt know the exact mechanics of how it works but I think of it as follows:

  1. We start with 100 tokens total supply on eth.
  2. You want to send 50 tokens to xDai.
  3. 50 tokens get locked in a contract on the eth side to mint 50 tokens on the xDai side.
  4. You end up with 50 available tokens + 50 locked tokens on eth and 50 available tokens on xDai.
  5. Your circulating supply of tokens is 50 tokens on eth and 50 tokens on xDai.
  6. Your total circulating token supply remain at 100 tokens.
  7. The 50 locked tokens on eth can only be freed when you lock up the same number of tokens on xDai, keeping the number of circulating tokens the same when you add circulating tokens from eth and xDai.
