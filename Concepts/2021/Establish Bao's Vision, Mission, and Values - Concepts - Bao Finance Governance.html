Establish Bao's Vision, Mission, and Values

Concepts
Varyints October 11, 2021, 10:22pm 1

What is this proposal?

Let’s define and vote to establish Bao’s vision, mission, and values to better guide decisions, work, and interactions with the broader community.

A clearly stated vision will attract contributors who are intrinsically motivated and it will become the theme of our social media communication and future marketing. Consistent messaging across multiple channels will give different audiences a single idea of what Bao is all about.

Definitions

What is a VISION statement?

It describes WHAT an organization desires to ACHIEVE in a 5+ year timeframe. It depicts what the org will look like in the future. It motivates and inspires. It describes a result/impact/achievement/goal.

What is a MISSION statement?

It explains in simple and concise terms an organization’s reason for existing. It is WHY the org exists.

What are VALUES?

They are beliefs that guide our actions and decisions at a high level and on an individual basis day to day.

Statements

Below I start the discussion with a draft for us to build on and rewrite together. The final result may be totally different after we discuss and edit.

OUR VISION

"Liquidity in any discrete and quantifiable event. This liquidity generates accurate, consumable data streams in the form of synthetics to move the power of ideas, information, and decisions from institutions to individuals."

OUR MISSION

"Build platform tools that let users and other protocols easily manage synthetic assets and asset interactions through a marketplace for data."

OUR VALUES

We believe in…

  • Democratization

    • Because institutions shouldn’t have power over people.
    • To achieve this Bao will make synths easy to create and use.
    • At Bao our community drives the project.

  • Empowerment

    • Because people want to make decisions for themselves.
    • To achieve this Bao enables any person to use synths to better manage their own personal risk.
    • At Bao we enable individuals in the community to develop governance proposals and start initiatives.

  • Security

    • Because data is only useful when it can be trusted.
    • To achieve this Bao makes security a priority in development so users can trust the protocol that governs data on the platform.
    • At Bao we protect the community from bad actors and look out for each other.

  • Accuracy

    • Because accuracy enables better decision making and inspires creativity.
    • To achieve this data streams will be vetted by everyone in an open marketplace.
    • At Bao we make the effort to understand others and make our ideas clear.

  • Creativity

    • Because each person’s contribution is unique and important.
    • Bao achieves this by enabling synth solutions that don’t yet exist for uses that haven’t been imagined.
    • At Bao we celebrate and encourage creative contributions (artwork, videos, new perspectives, and unanticipated contributions).

Next Step

Share your impressions. What are your ideas? Is this what we want to achieve? Are these our values? What other project or organization’s credo do you admire?

The community will connect with our vision, mission, and values if they are simply stated and easy to share. Our tweets, memes, interviews, and future marketing work can be informed by the statements we choose. We can also make them a regular part of our daily work and interactions.

We can add decentralization as a separate value or remove this one but it’s not a precise word for this particular value. This value is about accessibility. It’s about making something anybody can use. The distinction is important to me because there are a lot of decentralized platforms that are not accessible: they aren’t translated into other languages, they are opaque (not well described in what they do or how to use them), or they require a high level of expertise, specialized knowledge, or education to use (a financial literacy which many people in the world do not have). Perhaps Accessibility is a better word for this value.
