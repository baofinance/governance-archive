Definitions

What is a VISION statement?

It describes WHAT an organization desires to ACHIEVE in a 5+ year timeframe. It depicts what the org will look like in the future. It motivates and inspires. It describes a result/impact/achievement/goal.

What is a MISSION statement?

It explains in simple and concise terms an organization‚Äôs reason for existing. It is WHY the org exists.

What are VALUES?

They are beliefs that guide our actions and decisions at a high level and on an individual basis day to day.

Statements

OUR VISION

"Liquidity in any discrete and quantifiable event. This liquidity generates accurate, consumable data streams in the form of synthetics to move the power of ideas, information, and decisions from institutions to individuals."

OUR MISSION

"Build platform tools that let users and other protocols easily manage synthetic assets and asset interactions through a marketplace for data."
